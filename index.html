Your Questions, Answered

Why not just use ChatGPT or another general AI?

Moods.ai is a curated experience, not just an AI. We provide value in three key ways: 1) **Zero Friction:** It's a single step via email, fitting into your life without new apps or logins. 2) **Guaranteed Quality:** Our AI companions use expertly crafted prompts to deliver consistently insightful reflections. We've done the hard work for you. 3) **True Privacy:** A dedicated, ephemeral channel feels safer and more private than a general chat history.

If entries are deleted, how do Premium insights work?

Privacy is paramount. Insights and trend-tracking are strictly opt-in features for Premium users. When you enable archiving, your entries are stored securely with end-to-end encryption. You can purge your archive at any time. For all other users, the "delete after reflection" policy remains absolute.

How do you ensure the AI responses are safe and unbiased?

This is our highest priority. Our AI companions are not general-purpose chatbots. They are built on fine-tuned models and operate using carefully engineered prompts that are continuously audited for safety, bias, and helpfulness. We explicitly prohibit the AI from giving medical advice and focus solely on supportive, insightful reflection.

Is this a replacement for therapy?

Absolutely not. Moods.ai is a tool for self-reflection, not a medical service. It is not designed to diagnose or treat any mental health conditions. If you are struggling, please seek help from a qualified professional.
